Resume / CV Pack


Description
===========

High Quality, print ready (CMYK) Resume / CV Pack that includes: Resume template + Portfolio page, Letter, Envelope, 2 * Business card and Folder design.


Template Size
=============


Sizes are: 

- Resume template: 2 A4 pages
- Portfolio page : 1 A4 page 
- Letter: A4 
- Envelope: No. 10 standard
- Business card: 9 * 5 cm.
- Folder design: for A4 pages


Fonts Used
==========

The fonts used in the templates include:
	
	- PT Sans Narrow: https://www.google.com/fonts/specimen/PT+Sans+Narrow
	- Oswald: https://www.google.com/fonts/specimen/Oswald

Usage
=====

Open the PSD files with Adobe Photoshop CS6.

The files are in CMYK mode and 300dpi format.

The files are divided into logical sections and you just have to insert your text / duplicate sections if needed.

For every picture from the theme there is a shape (named image shape) and you just have to make a clipping mask between the picture and the shape.
